ABOUT US

OUR SERVICES

32 Years of Experience That Specialises in Creating Travel Services

We pride ourselves on the stringent measures we take in providing the safest and highly trusted travel products at the best price to our customers. Each and every Travel Expert at Travel Center is well aware about the measures taken in-house to ensure a safe and delightful travel experience for our customers. We at Travel Center work towards delivering well planned worldwide travel products that are unique, high in quality and assembled according to the desires of the customers, at incredible rates. Travel Center, being a leading Pan-European travel brand from the highly reputed Moresand Ltd with a cumulative travel experience of over three decades, is renowned for its most affordable and all-inclusive travel solutions. We optimally utilise our wide travel network that has been established over the years in order to meticulously connect our esteemed customers, from the United Kingdom with the rest of the world. Travel Center owns an ATOL certification issued by Civil Aviation Authority which guarantees that the majority of all our flight arrangements and flight-inclusive packages are financially protected. Though some of our holiday packages and other travel services provided by other third party suppliers are not considered under ATOL protection, we assure that each and every travel supplier that we handpick is a pioneer in the respective industry with a proven track record of well protected service delivery.

Travel Center is also an IATA accredited agent enabling the company to have access to all the leading international and domestic airlines that ensures fair and uniform standards in booking flights for our customers. This also allows our customers to book with us confidently. We have teamed up with an extensive network of over 150 leading airlines, 65,000 hotels, insurance companies and many other handpicked travel suppliers to bring our client base with the most impressive travel solutions. In addition, Travel Center prides itself on being the official travel agent for over 84 international airlines. We offer a range of travel solutions that cater to a vast array of customers – both in the corporate and leisure travel market. Our services include flights, hotel accommodation, airport parking facilities, travel insurance, sporting events and so on, thereby providing fully-fledged and hassle-free travel arrangements. We have been constantly improving our services and will continue to work towards adding more valuable and financially secure services to our product list in the years to come.
